Enterprise HRM & Ticketing Platform

The Helpdesk application is a comprehensive HRM and ticketing platform designed for organizations operating across multiple countries. It enables users from different companies and regions to securely log in, create, and manage support tickets.

Key features include multi-tenant support for various companies, secure authentication with role-based access control, comprehensive ticket management with real-time updates, and powerful reporting capabilities. The system streamlines communication between customers and support teams, ensuring efficient issue resolution and improved client satisfaction.

As the lead developer on this project, I implemented scalable architecture patterns and optimized UI-backend interactions, resulting in a 35% increase in operational efficiency and a 40% improvement in stakeholder satisfaction.
